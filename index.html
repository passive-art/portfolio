I am a 3D artist and animator. I enjoy working in the creative field. I touched upon 3D art 5 years ago whereas it has been an on and off thing but it has been 2 years since when I have gotten very serious in this field. I am working up to getting better than where I am now in terms of 3D art, which includes animation and realism. I can work on a diverse style of art, though my main theme is realism.

As for my background, I am a sound designer for both music and film. I create sounds through recording, sampling, and synthesizers. I also create 3D art projects which include the use of photoshop and other digital art softwares such as Blender to bring what's imaginable to reality. These are things that I dedicated myself to until this current day. I allocate my free time to these activities as there is nothing else more that can bring me this much joy than these two. Furthermore, I have programming experience and have dedicated my knowledge into creating websites and scripts (for Linux-based machines). I am self-taught, alongside learning from courses made by various professional instructors. It wasn't any university that has driven me towards constant development. It was my passion.

Throughout my professional career, I worked in IT and a bit of web development until I discovered that I want to do more with technology. With the knowledge I have, I'd rather use all of that in the creative arts. In other words, IT was never my thing. My passion truly lies in creative arts, which includes music and 3D animation.

I find that my core values truly aligns with these positions. Overall, I am hoping that someday I can get my foot through the door in the world of 3D art and graphic design.

Just a side note, some of these projects posted are experimental. Eventually, I will post more of my projects in greater quality for this showcase. That would include not only photos, but also videos.
